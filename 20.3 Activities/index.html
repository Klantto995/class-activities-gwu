RESTORE HOPE: ARKANSAS

OVERVIEW

Restore Hope: Arkansas is a non-profit organization that uses a collective impact model to bring together government and community services in order to follow their mission of reducing incarceration and the need for foster care. The organization ties together the community resources provided for the people and extends a helping hand to those in crisis.

PROBLEM

While the website acts as a great foundation for the messages and goals that it proclaims, it lacks any real substance related to actual resources or explanation of those resources that it provides which leaves a lot of open space for these opportunities.

SOLUTION

Apply UI components that provide a more aesthetically pleasing experience and add resource pages that provide helpful information and links to programs that are essential to the re-entry process for previously incarcerated individuals thus furthering the mission statement of Restore Hope.
